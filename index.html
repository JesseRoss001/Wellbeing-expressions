Our Mission & Philosophy

At Well Being Expression, our mission is to provide a safe and nurturing space where individuals can explore their emotions and experiences through art. We understand that life can be challenging, and we're here to support you on your journey towards improved mental health and well-being.

Our philosophy is rooted in the belief that creativity is inherent in everyone. Through holistic art therapy, we encourage self-expression without judgment, fostering a sense of empowerment and personal insight. We combine traditional therapeutic techniques with artistic practices to address emotional, psychological, and social well-being.

We are committed to inclusivity, accessibility, and community engagement. By collaborating with local organisations and tailoring our sessions to diverse groups, we aim to make a positive impact on the lives of individuals across Holyhead and Anglesey.

Meet May Berbery Canvin

Founder & Holistic Art Practitioner

Hello! I'm May, a passionate holistic art practitioner dedicated to helping others find healing and joy through creative expression. With a background in fine arts and psychology, I have always been fascinated by the intersection of art and mental well-being.

Currently pursuing a degree in Art Therapy, I bring a blend of academic knowledge and real-world experience to my practice. Over the past five years, I've worked with individuals of all ages, from children to the elderly, tailoring sessions to meet their unique needs.

My approach is compassionate, person-centred, and grounded in the understanding that everyone has the capacity for creativity. I believe that through art, we can access deeper parts of ourselves, gain new perspectives, and foster personal growth.

I'm excited to accompany you on your journey towards enhanced well-being.

The Benefits of Holistic Art Therapy

Reduces Stress & Anxiety

Engaging in creative activities can lower cortisol levels, helping you feel more relaxed and centred.

Enhances Self-Expression

Art provides a non-verbal outlet to express emotions and thoughts that may be difficult to articulate.

Boosts Self-Esteem

Creating art fosters a sense of accomplishment and can improve confidence in your abilities.

Promotes Social Connection

Group sessions encourage interaction and can help build supportive relationships with others.

Encourages Mindfulness

Art-making allows you to be present in the moment, enhancing awareness and reducing distractions.

Sparks Creativity

Exploring different art forms can inspire new ideas and ways of thinking, both in art and life.
