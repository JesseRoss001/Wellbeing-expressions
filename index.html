About Wellbeing Expression

Helping clients express themselves and find relief through creative outlets.

At Wellbeing Expression, we provide a safe, welcoming space where individuals can engage in various forms of creativity for well-being and mental health. Our sessions focus on creativity without the need for prior skills or expectations.

We offer a range of activities including visual arts, music, writing, pottery, and sensory-focused experiences designed to be relaxing and therapeutic.

About May

May is a dedicated HICAT (Holistic Integrated Creative Art Therapy) student based on Anglesey in North Wales.

Her passion is for integrating creativity and healing through all her work and mediums of expression. With a strong career background in listening, understanding, and responding to people’s needs, May has always had an aptitude for the creative arts and wanted to share this to help others – adults as well as youth - demonstrating her commitment to fostering wellbeing and self-expression through the arts.
