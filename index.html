About Wellbeing Expressions

Helping clients express themselves and find relief through creative outlets.

At Wellbeing Expressions, we provide a safe, welcoming space where individuals can engage in various forms of creativity for well-being and mental health. Our sessions focus on creativity without the need for prior skills or expectations.

We offer a range of activities including visual arts, music, writing, pottery, and sensory-focused experiences designed to be relaxing and therapeutic.

Workshops

Creative workshops for healing and exploration.

Our creative workshops are designed for everyone who wishes to heal any aspect of their mental, emotional, or physical well-being. Whether you want to explore your emotions or simply enjoy a creative outlet, our workshops provide the perfect environment.

We believe in creating inclusive environments that recognize and address unique challenges while promoting empowerment and dignity. By fostering compassion and enhancing accessibility, we aim to serve and uplift everyone, ensuring that all individuals can thrive.

Meet May Berbery Canvin

Founder & Holistic Art Practitioner

May is a dedicated HICAT (Holistic Integrated Creative Art Therapy) student based on Anglesey in North Wales. Her passion is integrating creativity and healing through all her work and mediums of expression.

With a strong career background in listening, understanding, and responding to people’s needs, May has always had an aptitude for the creative arts and wanted to share this to help others—adults as well as youth—demonstrating her commitment to fostering wellbeing and self-expression through the arts.
